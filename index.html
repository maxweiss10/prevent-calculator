Epic .PREVENT SmartPhrase

Build this once in SmartPhrase Manager (name it PREVENT). Then type .prevent in any note, fill it, select all, copy, and paste above. Keep the labels unchanged; change the values freely.


    

      Full auto pulls everything: @BRIEFLABS()@ for labs, and
      @PROB@ / @MEDS@ / @SOCIALHX@
      so the calculator can also detect diabetes, statin, antihypertensive, and smoking from your
      problem/med/social data — no typing. Anything auto-detected shows an amber dot
      and a "verify" note, since a med/problem list can't always convey intent (e.g. a β-blocker not used for BP).
      Labs only keeps the four Yes/No as *** wildcards (press F2 to jump).
      Blank or missing values just fall through to the editable form.
    

  


  

    
Model: AHA PREVENT equations (base + optional CKM/SDI). Coefficients from the
      Supplemental Appendix of Khan SS, Matsushita K, Sang Y, et al.
      “Development and Validation of the American Heart Association’s PREVENT Equations.”
      Circulation. 2024;149(6):430–449. This tool’s engine was ported from and verified
      bit-for-bit against the CRAN preventr
      package (v0.11.0) across 1,000 test cases (all 5 outcomes, both sexes, both horizons).

    
Scope: 10-year risk is validated for ages 30–79; 30-year risk for ages 30–59.
      ASCVD risk categories (low/borderline/intermediate/high) follow the 2018/2019 ACC/AHA thresholds,
      which were derived with the Pooled Cohort Equations — apply clinical judgment.

    
Disclaimer: For educational and clinician-support use only. Not a substitute for clinical
